About SmartFixTexas

Your trusted partner for home services in Texas

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SmartFixTexas was born out of a need for honest, reliable home service professionals in the Austin area. Our founders, Texas natives with over 20 years of combined experience in plumbing, HVAC, and electrical services, noticed that homeowners were often overcharged and underserved.

We set out to change that by creating a company that puts customers first, with transparent pricing, same-day service, and guaranteed quality workmanship.

Our Mission

At SmartFixTexas, our mission is simple: provide smart solutions that are fixed right the first time, with the strength and reliability that Texas homeowners deserve.

We believe in treating every home as if it were our own and every customer as if they were family. That means honest assessments, fair pricing, and work that stands the test of time.
